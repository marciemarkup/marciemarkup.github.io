What's the process?

Discovery

Research

Mockup

Launch!

Step 1: Discovery

Discovery Goals

I want to learn as much as I can about your business or personal brand!

I’ll ask you a bunch of questions to get a feel for your business goals and we’ll discuss how your future website can help you meet them.

What You'll Get

At the end of this step, you’ll receive a summary of what was discussed, as well as a preliminary Plan-of-Action.

Step 2: Research

Research Goals

I’ll conduct some further research into your niche of business.

I’ll review your competitor’s sites and find out what we can do to give you an edge!

What You'll Get

At the end of this step, you’ll receive a summary of my research, as well as a completed Plan-of-Action.

Step 3: Mockup

Mockup Goals

At this stage, I’ll design your website based on our Plan-of-Action!

Making a mockup first allows us to review what the site will look like and make changes quickly, as needed!

What You'll Get

At the end of this step, you’ll receive a Mockup of your website for review.

Step 4: Launch!

Launch Goals

It’s time to make the mockup come to life with a full, functioning website!

This is when everything comes together. I’ll create your website and help you launch it.

What You'll Get

At the end of this step, you’ll have your shiny, new website out on the web!
